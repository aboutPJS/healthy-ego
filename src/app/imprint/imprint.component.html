HEALTHY EGO

Arts- & Culture Association

We are coordinating collaborations between artists and creatives. The HEALTHY EGO association is connecting musicians, photographers, videographers, producers, graphic designers, software developers and fashion designers.

In addition, we offer artists a platform: by realising events, our exciting variety of projects can be experienced. In addition to organising exhibitions, concerts and readings, we are also supporting our association members in the conception and promotion of publications.

We are here to support young artists and creatives. Do you need support in realising your ideas? We are always on the lookout for talents who make our association even more diverse! We are location-independent - anyone can participate from anywhere.
Just contact us on Instagram or send us an e-mail. We are looking forward to hearing from you!
